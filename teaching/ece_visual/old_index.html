Assignment

Description:

Visual Computing course (EE5731R) has two parts, and this website is about the second part of the course. In this part, we focus on a few important topics in computer vision, namely, Bayesian inference, Markov Random Field (MRF), graphcuts, camera geometric properties, camera calibration, stereo, multiview geometry, and optical flow.

To complete the course, programming skill in Matlab isrequired.
