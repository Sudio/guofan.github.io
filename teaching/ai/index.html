Artificial Intelligence

Central South University





Description:

This course introduces students to the basic knowledge representation, problem solving, and learning methods of artificial intelligence. By learning the course, students should be able to develop intelligent systems by assembling solutions to concrete computational problems; understand the role of knowledge representation, problem solving, and learning in intelligent-system engineering; and appreciate the role of problem solving, vision, and decision making in understanding human intelligence from a computational perspective. This course focuses on the fundamentals of artificial intelligence. It should interest international students who want to study/work in artificial intelligence.


Prerequisite: Computer programming, data structure, Discrete Mathematics, probability, calculus and linear algebra. 
Textbooks: "Intelligent Control: Principles, Techniques and Applications", by Zixing Cai. 
Instructor: Fan Guo (fanguo [att] csu.edu.cn) (2013-2018)
